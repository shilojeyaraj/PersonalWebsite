Medieval Trebuchet Model

This project involved designing and building a scale model of a medieval trebuchet. The model was constructed using wood and metal components, and it was capable of launching projectiles over a significant distance. The project included detailed research on historical trebuchet designs and modern engineering principles to optimize the performance of the model.
